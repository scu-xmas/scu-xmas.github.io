20 of the best from 2020

It’s been an extraordinary year. We’ve been inspired by tales of adaptation, tales of excellence, of the world getting smaller, ideas getting bigger, people defying expectations and shining a light on what matters. As this truly weird year draws to a close, here are 20 stories from 2020 at Southern Cross University to make you think, smile and wonder what next year holds in store.

Scroll down for more

Month here?

Record numbers of students flock to Southern Cross

20,000+ students and growing! For the first session of 2020 we welcomed our largest cohort ever, a trend that continued through the year in both session 2 and 3. No prizes for guessing the most popular courses were in Health.

Month here?

We looked on the bright side

Southern Cross scientist Dr Dan Harrison hooked up a modified snow blower and blew vaporised water into the clouds above the Great Barrier Reef. It sounds like science fiction, but cloud brightening could help protect the reef from mass bleaching events.

Month here?

Fossil dating leads to rethink of human evolution

Meanwhile, our very own fossil dating expert Associate Professor Renaud Joannes-Boyau was part of a team that proved there were several species of human living in Africa 300,000 years ago. They’ve all disappeared – except us.

Month here?

New Health Sciences building gets first-year cohort through the doors

Our beautiful new multi-million-dollar Health Sciences building at Coffs Harbour signalled a new era of health education in this coastal paradise, with space for everything from exercise science and nursing to occupational therapy.

Month here?

COVID struck and everything moved online

It was all online but it wasn’t boring! Music students produced collaborative medieval chanting, lecturers delivered lectures on fish sampling from the field, social work students completed their training online and zoom became our new BFF.

We were starry-eyed

The latest round of the Good Universities Guide saw a number of 5-star rankings awarded to Southern Cross. Notably, we picked up number 1 in Australia for overall experience in environmental sciences, engineering and technology and agriculture.

Regenerative Agriculture postgrad course launches

In a year that underlined the importance of a global community working together, our Australia-first regenerative agriculture program launched a new course for postgraduate students.
